About Me

I'm a graduate from the University of Massachusetts Amherst. I majored in Mathematics and later went to acquire my FINRA series 7 and 63 to work in Finance. I assisted clientele with targeting retirement needs and investment options that were suited for them, depending on their financial situation. I retained and loved everything I learned, but decided to take my analytical skills and apply them to Software Engineering and Web Development. Through a 12-week, 500+ hours course at General Assembly, I acquired the skills and foundation I needed to become a Full-Stack Web Developer.

Coming from a strong Math and Science background, in addition to working in Finance for a few years, I truly believe no problem is too big to solve. I pride myself in staying calm while breaking down large problems into smaller ones, and solving them through a systematic approach to planning, critical thinking and attention to detail. Although I've learned so much, I'm STILL, very eager to about learning new technologies and adapting along with tech and software.

Software Engineering and Web Development are honestly so fascinating to me. Whether its through social media applications that have connected lost relationships or created new ones, or apps that simply make life more convenient, software has either vastly improved the way we do things or innovated new ways! Software and technology are going to be relevant forever and it's going to keep advancing. I'd like to be part of that advancement, and contribute as much as I possibly can!
